About Ivan

Ivan Byekwaso, born September 29, 1983 in Jinja, Uganda, began his athletic journey early through football. His dedication earned him educational bursaries and opportunities, including trials for Uganda’s U20 national team. His transformation into bodybuilding began while working as a security professional in Kampala, where he developed a strong physique and passion for fitness. Inspired by Arnold Schwarzenegger and Jean-Claude Van Damme, Ivan committed himself to discipline and training. He rose quickly, winning Mr. Uganda in the junior category at age 22. He became a record holder: 5x Mr Uganda Champion 4x Mr Kampala Champion International achievements include: - INBA/PNBA World Cup Winner (USA) - Natural Olympia Champion (Las Vegas) - Multiple international podium finishes Today, Ivan is a professional natural bodybuilder and coach helping clients achieve real transformation. Languages: English, German, Luganda, Kiswahili
