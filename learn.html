About Ivan Byekwaso

← Back to Home

Ivan Byekwaso, widely known as “Mr Uganda”, is a 5-time Mr Uganda Champion and internationally recognized natural bodybuilder. Born on September 29, 1983 in Jinja, Uganda, Ivan developed a passion for sports early, starting with football before transitioning into bodybuilding. His transformation began while working as a security professional in Kampala, where he developed his physique and passion. Inspired by Arnold Schwarzenegger and other legends, he committed to discipline and training.

Ivan became: • 5x Mr Uganda Champion • 4x Mr Kampala Champion • Record holder in Uganda International achievements: • World Cup Winner (USA 2024) • Natural Olympia Champion • Multiple international victories Today Ivan is an elite coach helping people achieve transformation. Languages: English, German, Luganda, Kiswahili.
