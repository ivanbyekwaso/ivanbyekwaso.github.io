About Ivan Byekwaso

Ivan Byekwaso, widely known as “Mr Uganda”, is a 5-time Mr Uganda Champion and internationally recognized natural bodybuilder. Born on September 29, 1983 in Jinja, Uganda, Ivan developed a passion for sports at an early age. He began playing football in primary school, earning bursaries that supported his education and eventually leading him to trial for the Uganda U20 National Team as a goalkeeper. His transformation into bodybuilding began while working as a security professional (bouncer) in Kampala. During this time, he developed an impressive physique, which became the foundation for his bodybuilding career. Inspired by legendary figures such as Arnold Schwarzenegger, Sylvester Stallone, and Jean-Claude Van Damme, Ivan committed himself fully to discipline and training.

At the age of 22, he entered his first bodybuilding competition — Mr Uganda — and won first place in the junior category. This marked the beginning of an extraordinary journey. Ivan went on to dominate: • 5x Mr Uganda Champion • 4x Mr Kampala Champion • Record holder in Uganda He later expanded internationally: • World Cup Winner (USA 2024) • Natural Olympia Champion (Las Vegas 2024) • Natural Universe Winner (USA 2015) • Multiple global podium finishes Today Ivan is a professional coach helping individuals achieve real transformation. He works with: • Beginners and advanced athletes • Men and women of all ages • People seeking weight loss, muscle gain, strength, endurance and flexibility Training is flexible: home, gym, outdoors. Languages: English, German, Luganda, Kiswahili.
